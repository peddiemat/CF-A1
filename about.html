About Me

I started theatre when I was in the fourth grade. My teacher wrote short plays and we would perform them for the rest of the school in the cafeteria. I enjoyed performing; however, there was something missing. I would always notice people working, but I had no clue what they were doing. It was not until the eighth grade that I figured out what they were doing. It was at that point I found my calling.


I am a graduate of the Cobb County Center for Excellence in the Performing Arts at Pebblebrook High School with a major in Technical Theatre. I work around the greater Atlanta area as a freelance Master Electrician and Lighting Designer. I recently received my membership card for I.A.T.S.E. Local 927.
